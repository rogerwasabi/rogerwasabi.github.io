About Us

YANO A.I Technologies Inc. is a cutting-edge A.I school dedicated to empowering the next generation of innovators. Founded in 2023, we blend theoretical knowledge with practical applications in artificial intelligence.

Our mission is to democratize A.I education, making it accessible and engaging for everyone from beginners to experts.

Our Vision

  • Foster a community of tech enthusiasts
  • Bridge the gap between academia and industry
